Flagship work

Volatility Cascade Engine

A simulation of leverage, margin breach, forced liquidation, and secondary price impact in overlapping portfolios. The point is not forecasting. The point is making threshold behavior visible.

Implemented Python Simulation

Losses stay linear until the structure stops cooperating.

The system looks contained under smaller shocks. Around a 16% initial shock, the first distressed fund appears. By 20%, there are three distressed funds and materially steeper aggregate loss. That inflection is the core result.
