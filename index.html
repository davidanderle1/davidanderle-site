Primary recommendation Finance Networks

Systemic financial risk modeling

TL;DR: Build a network-based project that models how shocks spread across connected financial institutions and how systemic fragility becomes visible only after pressure rises.

  • Problem: Most balance-sheet views miss how contagion propagates through connections.
  • Approach: Network models, stress scenarios, and interpretable systemic-risk metrics.
  • Expected output: Visualized contagion paths, risk rankings, scenario tables, and a reproducible notebook.
Why this is primary: it matches the site’s intellectual identity, has strong finance relevance, and can become a visible proof-of-thinking project.
