About

I am a student at the University of Wisconsin–Madison with a background in informatics from CZU Prague.

I spend most of my time working on problems related to financial markets, risk, and decision-making.

What interests me is not theory for its own sake, but situations where things can go wrong: high volatility, limited information, and pressure to act anyway.

This site is a record of what I am building and thinking about over time.
