AIFinanceMulti-agent systems

AI-Driven Hedge Fund Research Assistant

A multi-agent investment research platform combining LLMs, retrieval pipelines, structured financial data, and specialized agents for summarization, quantitative reasoning, and thesis generation.

Why it is impressive

It turns current frontier AI into a finance-native research workflow rather than a single chatbot wrapper. The project signals orchestration thinking, applied ML depth, and relevance to real investment research.

What to build

  • Financial documents corpus, price history, and macro data layer.
  • RAG stack, agent router, and evaluation harness.
  • Backtests comparing generated research signals against benchmarks.
  • Interactive demo showing agent chain, sources, and confidence.
Timeline: 6-9 monthsTeam: 1-2 ML, 1 quant, 1 data engineer

Graph MLSystemic risk

Graph Neural Systemic-Risk Engine

A graph learning platform that models contagion across financial institutions, stress-tests networked balance sheets, and predicts cascades or capital shortfalls under shock scenarios.

Signal

Combines network science, contagion logic, and modern graph architectures in a domain elite evaluators instantly recognize as hard and consequential.

Build

  • Interbank exposure datasets plus synthetic stress-generation pipeline.
  • GNN and permutation-equivariant baselines.
  • Contagion dashboard with animated shock paths.
Timeline: 9-12 monthsAudience: quants, regulators
