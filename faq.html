FAQ

Frequently asked questions about David Anderle, his work, and the purpose of the site.

Who is David Anderle?

David Anderle is a University of Wisconsin–Madison exchange student and a Czech University of Life Sciences Prague Informatics student focused on systems, risk, financial systems, and decision-making under uncertainty.

What does David Anderle work on?

He works on systems under stress, financial-system fragility, decision quality under uncertainty, policy questions related to markets, and technical projects that require evidence rather than branding.

Where does David Anderle study?

He studies Informatics at the Czech University of Life Sciences Prague and is completing exchange study at the University of Wisconsin–Madison.

What is David Anderle’s flagship project?

The flagship build is a systemic financial risk model designed to show how shocks propagate across connected institutions and where fragility hides before pressure rises.

The best direct contact is david.anderle1@gmail.com. LinkedIn and GitHub are also linked from the official website.
